Eko Rudiawan Jamzuri (姜奧開)


I am a Lecturer in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Politeknik Negeri Batam, Indonesia. I am also a member of BRAIL (Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab) who works on Humanoid Robot and Computer Vision research. You can find an overview of my research through my YouTube Channel or Github Repository.


I got Master's Degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan Normal University in 2020. Previously, I graduated from the School of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, Bandung Institute of Technology in 2013. In my spare time, I love traveling to a new place and enjoy outdoor sport (hiking, cycling, running, and sometimes swimming). Of course, I also like to write code with Python and drink more cups of coffee in the morning.


More about me can be found through my Curriculum Vitae.
